What Can Go Wrong (And How to Fix It)

Problem 1: Forget to Reconfirm TFC

Impact: Lose £2,000/year

Fix: Set phone reminder every 3 months. TFC emails you but easy to miss.

Problem 2: Income Crosses £100k

Impact: Lose TFC + 30 Hours Free (£8,840/year)

Fix: Salary sacrifice to drop back under £100k. This ALSO saves tax.

Problem 3: Change Jobs

Impact: Forget to update TFC, account gets closed

Fix: Update within 14 days of job change. Add to resignation checklist.

Problem 4: Junior ISA Provider Fees Too High

Impact: Lose £3,000 to fees over 18 years

Fix: Switch to Vanguard (0.15% fee). Can transfer existing ISA.

Problem 5: Stop Contributions in Market Crash

Impact: Miss buying opportunity, lower returns

Fix: KEEP contributing. Market crashes = discount prices. You have 18 years.
